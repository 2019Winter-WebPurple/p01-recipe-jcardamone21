Gluten-Free Banana Oat Waffles

Are you gluten-free but still love breakfast foods? Are you looking for a new waffle recipe? Or do you just love breakfast no matter what it's made of? If so, this recipe is for you! These Gluten-Free Banana Oat Waffles are easy to make, delicious, and wholesome!

Ingredients!

  • 2¼ cups oat flour
  • 3 tablespoons packed cocnut sugar or brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons arrowroot starch or corn starch
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk of choice
  • Scant ½ cup melted coconut oil or 7½ tablespoons butter, melted
  • ¾ cup mashed ripe bananas, about 2 medium
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • Suggested toppings: thinly sliced banana, maple syrup or honey, nut butter and/or toasted nuts, or a whipped cream of tour choice

    • Instructions!
    1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the oat flour, sugar, starch, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon. Whisk to combine.
    2. In a medium mixing bowl, whisk the eggs. Then add the milk, coconut oil or butter, mashed banana and vanilla extract. Whisk until the mixture is throughly blended.
    3. Pour the liquid mixture into the dry mixture. Stir with a big spoon until combined (the batter will have a few lumps). Let the batter rest for 10 minutes so the oat flour has time to soak up some of the moisture. Plug in your waffle iron to preheat now.
    4. Once ten minutes is up, give the batter one more, gentle swirl with your spoon. The batter will be pretty thick. Using a measuring cup, pour batter onto the heated waffle iron, enough to cover the center and most of the central surface area, and close the lid.
    5. Wait to check on the waffles until most of the steam has stopped billowing out the sides (this takes about 5-6 minutes). Once the waffle is deeply golden and crisp, transfer it to a cooling rack or baking sheet. Don't stack your waffles on top of each other, or they'll lose crispness.
    6. If desired, keep your waffles warm by placing them in a 200 degree oven until you're ready to serve. Repeat with remaining batter and serve with desired toppings.
    7. Enjoy!
